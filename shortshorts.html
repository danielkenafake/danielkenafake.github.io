What???

Who likes short shorts? Short Shorts is a short film festival for friends, by friends! We'll catch up, eat too much, and cheers to what we love most: film.


There's only one catch. To attend you MUST submit a film. Quality, effort, production value, entirely your choice.

Theme:

Fish. Interpret this however you like. 🐟

Max Duration:

1 Minute.

Format:

Primarily live-action, animated elements are welcome. Creative extras and collaboration are strongly encouraged. Chaos is welcome.

When?!

Submissions are due:

25th October 2025

Screening:

The screening will take place on 14th November 2025

Screening:

More info:

  • Awards will be voted on and presented on the night. As part of the submission we ask you to suggest a category for consideration. Example: The "Shot on iPhone but Make it Cannes" Award, Best Performance by an Inanimate Object, The "You Good?" Award.
  • Any issues submitting, please email danielkenafake@gmail.com
  • Submitting an entry counts as your RSVP to attend on the evening.
  • Please film in landscape, not portrait
  • All you have to lose is your dignity and the respect of your peers!
